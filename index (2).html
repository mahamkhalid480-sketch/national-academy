Our Story

Our Journey

From a small dream in Arifwala to a transformative institution — shaping futures with passion, purpose, and cutting-edge education.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional education and tomorrow's demands, National Academy of Science/Arts has become a trusted institution in Arifwala. With 4+ years of hands-on teaching experience, our dedicated team ensures no student — especially girls — is left behind in the digital age.

We believe education must be future-based: blending AI, coding, ethics, and Quran — because true success is holistic.

Beginning
🌱 The Dream Begins
Started with a passion for quality education in Arifwala. Launched foundational computer and English courses for underserved students.
Growth
📈 Expanding Our Reach
Introduced Nazra/Quran alongside academics. Built an ethics-first curriculum. Students began achieving outstanding board results.
Innovation
🤖 Entering the AI Era
Launched Python, AI & Chatbot courses — one of the first academies in the region to teach hands-on AI to school-level students.
Today · 2026
🚀 A Complete Academy
Pre-Level to Bachelor's programs, 4 expert teachers, online learning for above-9th class, and a dedicated safe space for girls' education.
